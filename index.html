The Premise

What if geometry is the substrate?

What if the geometry of spacetime isn't just the container for consciousness — but its substrate? 4DGeo explores the hypothesis that topological invariants of 4-dimensional geometric structures correspond to measurable properties of conscious experience.

This isn't mysticism dressed in math. It's the same persistent homology and sheaf cohomology used in the ATFT framework for the Riemann Hypothesis — applied to the question of how structure gives rise to awareness. If discrete topology is fundamental and continuous fields emerge as macroscopic limits, then the boundary between physics and consciousness may be a filtration parameter, not a wall.

The persistent features that survive across all scales of observation — in a brain, in a field configuration, in a mathematical structure — are the topological invariants. Shape over count. The question is whether consciousness is one of those invariants.
